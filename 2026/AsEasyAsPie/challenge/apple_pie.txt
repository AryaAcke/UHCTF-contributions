Ingredients:

- 6 cups sliced apples (about 5 medium)
- 2/3 cup sugar
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 double pie crust (store-bought or homemade)
- 1 egg (for egg wash, optional)

Steps:

1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).

2. Toss apples with sugar, flour, cinnamon, and lemon juice.

3. Line a 9" pie dish with bottom crust, fill with apple mixture, top with second crust (vent or lattice). Brush with beaten egg if desired.

4. Bake 20 minutes at 425°F, then reduce to 375°F (190°C) and bake 25–30 more minutes until filling bubbles and crust is golden. Cool at least 30 minutes before slicing.
